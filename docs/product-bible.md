# Kahu Product Bible (Snapshot)

> Working docs can live in Notion/Google Docs; this file is the “frozen” reference for designers, engineers, and AI tools.

## 1. Mission & Vision

- **Mission:** Help humans become confident, compassionate guardians for their dogs through evidence-based training and tools.
- **Vision:** A global companion app for dog guardianship – covering training, health, and everyday care, deeply integrated with vets and trusted partners.

## 2. One-liner

Kahu is an AI-powered dog training and wellness app that helps you raise a happy, well-adjusted dog and stay on top of health and care with support from vets and trusted services.

## 3. Target Users

- New dog guardians
- Prospective dog guardians (pre-purchase)
- Experienced guardians solving behaviour issues
- Vet clinics and professionals

## 4. Core Pillars

1. Training
2. Health & Records
3. Pre-Purchase Education
4. Connected Care (vets, walkers, groomers)
5. Notifications & Guidance

## 5. Territories

- Initial: New Zealand, Australia
- Future: US, UK, EU and other high-LTV markets
